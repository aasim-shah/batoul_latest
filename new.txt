Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: How do I create an account on your platform?
A: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the page. Fill in the required information, including your email address and a secure password. Additionally, providing your National ID is mandatory for verification purposes to ensure a secure and trustworthy platform.

Q: Can I add my property or showcase my interior design work on the platform?
A: Absolutely! Once you are logged in and have added your National ID, you can easily add your property or showcase your interior design work. Navigate to the appropriate section, fill in the necessary details, such as property information or design portfolio, and submit it for review. Your listing or work will be visible to potential clients and interested users.

Q: How can I contact property owners or interior designers?
A: To ensure a secure and reliable platform, users can view the contact information, including phone numbers, of property owners or interior designers. This allows for direct communication using platforms like WhatsApp.

Q: Can I create wishlists for properties I'm interested in?
A: Absolutely! Once you are logged in, you can create wishlists to save properties you are interested in. This allows you to keep track of your preferred options and easily access them later.

Q: How secure is my personal information on your platform?
A: We prioritize the security and privacy of our users. All personal information, including your National ID, is encrypted and stored securely. We adhere to strict data protection measures to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your information.

If you have any further questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our support team. We're here to help you make the most of your experience on our platform.
